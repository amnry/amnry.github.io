Introduction

Hi there! I'm Aman Arya, currently pursuing a Master's in Data Science at Stony Brook University.
With a strong foundation in machine learning, quantitative research, and model validation, I'm passionate about exploring the dynamic world of financial mathematics and quantitative finance. My research interests span a broad spectrum, including Financial Risk Management, Stochastic Modelling, Portfolio Optimization, and Financial Economics.

Before diving into the realm of data science and finance, I earned an integrated BS-MS degree from Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Mohali, where I specialized in astrophysics. My master's research, conducted under the guidance of Prof. Annapurni Subramaniam at Indian Institute of Astrophysics, Bangalore, involved deriving insights from vast astrophysical datasets. This experience honed my mathematical skills and sparked my interest in applying these techniques to real-world problems, ultimately leading me to the field of financial mathematics.
